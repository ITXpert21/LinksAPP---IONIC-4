FAQS
Can I join The Links?
Membership to The Links, Incorporated are by invitation only. Please contact the Membership Department at The Links Headquarters by calling 202-842-8686 or by emailing membership@linksinc.org
How do I find out more information about The Links?
Please visit our website at www.linksinc.org
If my membership to The Links is currently inactive but I wish to return to Action, what do I need to do?
Please contact the Membership Department at The Links Headquarters by calling 202-842-8686 or by emailing membership@linksinc.org
What Chapters are near me?
You can view all the chapters that are near you based on your zip code and radius by visiting http://mobileapp.linksincdb.org/locator/locator.php
I would like the National President to speak at my function. How do I make that request?
You can make a request for the National President at http://www.linksinc.org/national-president-request. You will be contacted as soon as possible with availability and other information.
Where are your headquarters located?
